Material Handling Solutions

Deccan Material Handling solutions are crafted for tough handling conditions with ease. These rubberized heavy duty crates, utility crates and baskets are uniquely designed to make them extremely strong for roughest and toughest usage. Deccan Material Handling solutions have diverse applications in fishery, food processing, Agro industry, retail shops, and many other storing and transporting applications.

Key product features are:

  • Made from Prime virgin food grade HDPE and PP in ISO 9001:2010 certified facility.
  • Computer aided product and mold designs.
  • Lightweight and easy to handle yet Tough and long lasting.
  • Crack, chip and corrosion resistant.
  • Environmental stress and UV resistant.
  • Withstand temperatures from -35°C to 75°C.
  • Hygienic and non-toxic. Resist bacterial/ fungal growth.
  • Improve efficiency, hygiene and stop wastage.
  • Extremely economical in the long run.
  • Available in a range of pleasing colors.
