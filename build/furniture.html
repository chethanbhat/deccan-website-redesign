Furniture Solutions

Plastic Molded Furniture is an important part in improving the functional and aesthetic appeal of any ambiance, let it be it your commercial or your personal space. Putting this belief to work, we launched our furniture range in 2011. Technologically superior products, with a sharp focus on quality, colors and finish, makes Deccan Plast really functional and practical solutions in your day to day life.

Key Features are :

  • Molded in Prime virgin plastic raw materials
  • Computer aided product and mold designs
  • Rubberized with performance enhancing additives and Select color pigments
  • Tough and long lasting.

We offer most premium range of plastic molded furniture to choose from for individual liking and needs. Deccan Plast Quality Molded Solutions consists of a vast array of plastic molded furniture, like Premium Cabinets, Premium novelty chairs, Relax chairs, Premium Arm chairs, Ornamental chairs, Premium Dining Chairs, Midback arm chairs, Dining chairs, Armless chairs, Seating systems, Multipurpose tables, Trolley, Dining tables, Stools, Chairs for children, Rocker Toy for children and many more.
