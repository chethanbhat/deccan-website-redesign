Welcome to Deccan Plast Industries

Founded in 1992, Deccan Group is an acknowledged initiator and innovator of coastal Karnataka's plastics industry. No wonder that Deccan Plast Industries were first to offer the widest and most comprehensive Quality Molded Solutions.The Deccan Plast Industries has an advanced “state of the art” factory in Mangaluru, constructed in the standards matching any MNC factory having latest injection molding machines and molds and other equipment, is powered by latest technology complemented by in-house R & D and product development,

Our Premium Range of Products

We offer most premium range of plastic molded furniture to choose from for individual liking and needs. Deccan Plast Quality Molded Solutions consists of a vast array of plastic molded furniture, like Premium Cabinets, Premium novelty chairs, Relax chairs, Premium Arm chairs, Ornamental chairs, Premium Dining Chairs, Midback arm chairs, Dining chairs, Armless chairs, Seating systems, Multipurpose tables, Trolley, Dining tables, Stools, Chairs for children, Rocker Toy for children and many more.

We also have Material Handling Solutions like crates and baskets to facilitate storing and logistics of a very vast range of industrial and consumer products.
